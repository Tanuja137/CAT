KFCs All in One Bucket is a non-vegetarian meal that serves 4 people. It includes 1 leg, 1 Hot & Crispy, 2 wings, 2 strips, 1 Zinger fillet, 3 dips (20gm each) and 1 Pepsi 475 ml1. The standard bucket of chicken at KFC comes with 8 pieces of chicken. You also have additional bucket options that include a 12-piece bucket or 16-piece bucket
